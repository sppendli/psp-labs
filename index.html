The Problem

You have data. You don't trust it.

The same KPI shows different values in different reports. Every leadership meeting starts with someone reconciling numbers before anything else gets discussed. Sometimes the ERP says one thing, the Power BI dashboard says another, and someone pulls out an Excel sheet to settle it. Decisions slow down. Or worse, they get made on the wrong data.

Root cause The issue isn't your dashboards. It's the absence of a reporting foundation underneath them. Without a structured data model and defined business logic, every report is an island and every number is a question.

  • Operations-driven businesses — retail, supply chain, manufacturing, logistics

  • No dedicated internal analytics team
